Oscars Nominations

Enter the year, category, nominee, and/or info to get the nominations. You can also enter the number of times a nominee was nominated or won, and whether the nominee won or not.

Click on the "Get Nominations" button to get the nominations, and the "Get Nominees" button to get the nominees.

Click on the "Clear Input" button to clear the input fields, and the "Clear Output" button to clear the output.
